Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

"Every cup is a moment worth savoring."

— Street 1 Cafe

Tucked into the heart of Islamabad, Street 1 Cafe was born from a simple idea — that a great cup of coffee deserves an equally great space. Since 2018, we've been crafting drinks with care, sourcing single-origin beans, and building a corner of the city where people actually want to stay a while.

We're not just a cafe. We're a community space — for early risers, late workers, first dates, and old friends. Every detail, from the warm lighting to the handwritten menu, is designed to make you feel at home on Street 1.
